Meet the Team

Our Mission

To provide Jimny followers a simple and easy to use website where they can learn more about the latest generation as well as the history of Suzuki Jimny. This is also a place where guests can connect with other members to share their thoughts and ideas with others.

Our Vision

Our goal and promise to our most valued patrons:

To put forth our best efforts to provide a one stop website to know everything there is to know about Jimny. In the near future our guests will be able to share stories and photos of their personal adventures, upgrades and maintenance which will make our website a wealth a knowlege users can use for reference.
